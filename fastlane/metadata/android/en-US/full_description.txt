Podometer is a privacy-first step counter for Android that tracks your daily walking activity — with zero data leaving your device.

No network permission. No analytics. No tracking. Your activity data belongs to you.

FEATURES

Step Counting
Podometer counts your steps using the hardware step sensor built into modern Android devices. When the dedicated step sensor is unavailable, the app falls back to the raw accelerometer to ensure reliable counting across a wide range of hardware.

Flexible Step Goals
Set a daily step goal that fits your lifestyle, or configure minimum, target, and stretch tiers to give yourself meaningful milestones throughout the day. Rest days let you preserve your streaks without pressure.

Weekly Trends
A weekly bar chart gives you a clear view of your step history so you can spot patterns, stay motivated, and track progress over time.

Data Export
Export your step data in JSON format for personal analysis or backup. You remain in full control of your data.

Privacy by Design
- No INTERNET permission — the app cannot make network requests
- No account required — no sign-up, no login
- All data stored locally using Room and DataStore
- Minimal permissions: activity recognition, foreground service, and notifications — nothing else

Material 3 Interface
Built with Jetpack Compose and Material 3, Podometer provides a clean, modern interface that respects your system theme and display settings.
