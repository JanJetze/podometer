Place PNG screenshots of the app in this directory.

F-Droid displays these on the app's store listing page. Screenshots must be taken
from a running build of the app. Recommended resolutions follow the Material
Design guidelines for phone screenshots (e.g. 1080x1920 or similar 9:16 ratio).

Suggested screens to capture:
- Dashboard with today's step count and activity badge
- Activity timeline showing walking and cycling segments
- Weekly chart screen
- Data export / settings screen
